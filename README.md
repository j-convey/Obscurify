## Why Obscurify?

You own your music. You've organized it perfectly in Plex. Now enjoy it with a sleek, modern player that feels as good as your favorite streaming app.

**Desktop or mobile. Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, or Android. One app. Your entire library.**

## Get Started in 3 Steps

### 1. Download Obscurify
Get the app for your platform:
- **Windows**: Download the installer
- **macOS**: Download the DMG
- **Linux**: Download the AppImage
- **iOS**: Coming soon to the App Store
- **Android**: Coming soon to Google Play

### 2. Connect Your Plex Server
Launch the app and sign in with your Plex account. Obscurify will automatically find your music libraries.

<!-- Screenshot: Server connection screen -->

### 3. Start Listening
That's it. Your music is ready. Browse, search, and play.

<!-- Screenshot: First play experience -->

## Requirements

- A Plex Media Server with music libraries
- A Plex account (free or Plex Pass)
- Internet connection to stream from your server
